# Marketing Outreach Templates

Based on the proposed strategy for Surplus Funds Recovery.

## 1. Email Outreach Template
**Tone**: Informational, calm, non-threatening, compliant.

**Subject**: Notification regarding your former property at {{Address}}

**Body**:
Dear {{Name}},

I am writing to share some information that many homeowners are unaware of following a foreclosure sale.

When a property is sold at auction, the lender is only legally entitled to recover the balance of the debt owed to them. If the property sold for more than that amount, the remaining balance—known as "surplus funds"—legally belongs to the former owner.

We are surplus funds recovery specialists, and our goal is to help former homeowners determine if they are entitled to any of these excess proceeds.

**Our Process:**
*   **Free Review**: We research county records to see if a surplus exists.
*   **No Upfront Fees**: We only get paid if you successfully recover your funds.
*   **Simple Next Steps**: We handle the documentation and follow-up.

If you would like us to perform a free eligibility review for your property at {{Address}}, please visit our website or reply to this email.

[Link: Check Your Eligibility]

Sincerely,

## 2. Text Message Template
**Tone**: Short, curiosity-based, non-spammy.

**Better Version**:
"Hi {{Name}}, you may be entitled to surplus funds after the foreclosure sale of your property at {{Address}}. Would you like a free review to see if money is owed to you? [Link]"

**Avoid**:
*   "URGENT: Act now!"
*   "Guaranteed money waiting for you!"
*   "Claim your cash today!"
